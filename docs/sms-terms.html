Unnamed CRM sends one-time passcodes (OTP) to authorized users for the sole purpose of verifying sign-in to our internal CMS. No marketing or promotional messages are sent under this program.

You may opt in by entering your mobile number during account creation and checking the consent box on the sign-up form. The checkbox is not pre-selected. By opting in, you agree to receive OTP text messages for login verification.

3) Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your login activity.

Message and data rates may apply. Check your mobile plan for details.

5) Commands
