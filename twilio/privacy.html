Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-08-12

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Unnamed CRM handles personal information for our internal CMS users, including information collected for our SMS 2FA program.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: name, username, email, role, and related profile details.
  • Security & Auth Data: mobile number for OTP delivery, login timestamps, verification events, and device/IP metadata.
  • Operational & Support Data: logs, application telemetry, and communications with support.

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and secure access to the CMS (e.g., send OTP codes).
  • Operate, maintain, and improve security and reliability of our services.
  • Provide user support and respond to inquiries.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and audit obligations.

SMS Program Privacy

We do not sell or share mobile numbers or SMS consent with third parties/affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Limited sharing with service providers (e.g., messaging vendors) may occur solely to deliver and support the program, under contracts that restrict use.

How We Share Information

  • Service Providers/Subprocessors: cloud hosting, messaging, monitoring, and support partners bound by confidentiality and data-use limits.
  • Legal/Compliance: if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity of our systems.
  • Internal Access: limited to personnel with a legitimate business need, subject to access controls and policies.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide services, meet security, audit, and legal requirements, and then delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention policies.

Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information we process, including access controls, encryption in transit where applicable, and logging/monitoring.

Your Choices

  • SMS Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. You may still need to use other authentication methods to access the CMS.
  • Updates: You can request corrections to your account information via support@yourdomain.com.

International & Regional Notices

This internal program is intended for U.S. users. If local law grants additional rights, you may contact us to exercise them where applicable.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
